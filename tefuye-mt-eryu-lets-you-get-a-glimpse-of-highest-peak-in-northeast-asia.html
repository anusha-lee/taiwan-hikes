Take the Challenging Part to See the Highest Peak in Northeast Asia

Tefuye trail locates in Alishan National Scenic Area Administration in south Taiwan, and Mt. Ali or Alisha, is one of the most beautiful attractions in Taiwan. If you arrange this hike, you probably head south to Chiayi from Taipei and spend one night around here like us.
After getting off our hired bus in Zhizhong 自忠 around 2,288 m/ 7,507 ft, we were amazed by the views between in the valleys. Our guide told us this is a great spot to see the beautiful sunrise and you can camp nearby. If the haze and the mountains we witnessed at that moment was already astonishing, I can’t imagine how stunning it will be if I get a chance to see the sunrise.
