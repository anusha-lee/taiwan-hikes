Read those first

  • Most of the hiking trips are free. Hiking associations will arrange the transportations for some remote or multiple-day hikes, and there will be cost to pay. Therefore, participants need to sign up in advance.
  • Some hiking associations like Taiwan Mountain LOHAS Association require participants to sign up in advance for carpooling or guide arrangements for some free guided hikes.
  • For 100 Peaks hikes, please contact the associations or contact Anusha for more details.
  • Difficulty levels are rated by each hiking associations and for reference only. Some may be more challenging or easier depending on participants’ fitness.
  • English description may be weird because I use Google Translate or DeepL Free Translator to translate the hiking trips written in Chinese.
  • For more details or further inquiries, please contact with the hiking associations or contact Anusha for help.
