Note:

Day 1: 20 km, based on my hike on January 1, 2021

06:32 Arrived at Sunhai Bridge, the starting point of Danda Forest Road, got off the car, walked accross the temporary bridge, and got back to the car again
07:15 Our cars arrived at No. 14 Forest Compartment Outpost at Danda Working Circle (the Second Line Office) at 4.5k of Danda Forest Road. The Forest Bureau staff checked our documents.
07:35 Started our long hike
08:22 Saw sign of 54 on Danda Forest Road to Qitsai Lake
09:02 Reached the old Third Line Office at 8k
10:37 Reached Qanituan Tribe post 加年端社
11:03 Passed sign of 66
11:32 40-minute lunch break
15:08 Passed Takivatan 丹群
15:27 Arrived at the Six Line Office and called it a day
Total time: 7 hours 52 minutes, including long breaks

Day 2: 27 km, based on my hike on January 2, 2021

04:00 Got up
05:30 Started the hike
07:13 Reached the fork and took the right one up
09:31 Encounter the big rock slope at 30k on Danda Forest Road and took the shortcut to save 8 km
11:00 Had a 40-minute lunch break
12:20 Got back to Danda Forest Road, the third tunnel, 44.6k on Danda Forest Road
13:17 Saw sign of 100
13:56 Passed the fork to Taipower Guest House, 50.4k
15:01 Saw sign of 105
16:29 Passed the fork to Mt. Liushun
16:34 Arrived at the campsite near Qitsai Lake, 56.7k
16:45 Started descending to Qitsai Lake and draw water there
17:00 Reached Qitsai Lake and spent some time there before getting back to the campsite
Total time: 11 hours 04 minutes, including long breaks

Day 3: 17.5 km, based on my hike on January 3, 2021

6:00 Saw the amazing sunrise at Qitsai Lake
7:00 Left campsite for Mt. Liushun
07:06 Reached the fork to Mt. Liushun
08:25 Hiked along the ridgeline of the third southern section of Taiwan’s Central Mountain Range 南三段
10:00 Reached Mt. Liushun and took a break
11:25 40-minute lunch break
14:21 Reached the old railway track to Taipower Gaodeng Contact Station 台電高登聯絡站 and took a break
14:34 Took Tian Stairs 天梯
16:28 Reached Lovers’ Suspension Bridge 情人吊橋, the end of Wangrong Forest Road 萬榮林道47.3k
16:56 Reached Jiuzu Cabin 九族山莊 at Wongrong Forest Road 46k, and our guide tried to make contact with the satellite phone
19:30 Reached Adao Cabin 阿道別墅 at Wongrong Forest Road 42k and camp there
Total time: 12 hours 30 minutes, including long breaks

Day 4: 33.8 km, based on my hike on January 4, 2021

02:30 Got up
04:00 Left Adao Cabin and continue to hike on Wongrong Forest Road
07:23 Passed 36k sign also saying no cars allowed
09:11 Saw 30k sign
11:48 Took a lunch break at 21k + 300 sign
14:42 Reached 14k sign and got a glimpse of East Rift Valley
14:59 Passed Wanshan Temple at 12.65k
15:43 Reached the checkpoint at 9.3k and finished the hike
Total time: 11 hours 43 minutes, including long breaks
