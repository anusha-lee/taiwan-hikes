Distance: 12.2 km (7.6 miles). The distance from the other trailhead to Tongluo Train Station side is 2.2 km.
Brief itinerary: Sanyi Train Station (Height 266 meters) ➔ Mt. Sanjiao entrance (Height around 336 meters) ➔ Mt. Sanjiao (Height 567 meters) ➔ Mt. Changkeng (aka Mt. Sanjiao North Peak) (Height 560 meters) ➔ Mt. Shuangfeng (Height 538 meters) ➔ Mt. Shuangfeng North Peak (Height 450 meters) ➔ Mt. Tungyuan (Height 339 meters) ➔ Tongluo Train Station (Height 185 meters)
GPS coordinates of parking lot near Mt. Sanjiao: 24.421786, 120.779057
GPS coordinates of the trailhead near Tongluo Train Station: 24.473855, 120.796251

Note:
My GPS track may not be totally accurate and is for reference only.

Based on my hike on October 25, 2020

09:48 Left Sanyi Train Station
09:49 Turned at underpass with the sign pointing to Mt. Sanjiao
09:53 Saw the map and entered the tunnel
10:02 Turned to a parking lot and saw the entrance of steps with a map of Mt. Sanjiao, Mt. Shuangfeng, and Tung Flower Trail
10:10 Saw a fork and turned to the right
10:28 Reached the steep slope with ropes
10:35 Passed the power tower
10:39 Reached Mt. Sanjiao and started hiking on the ridge
11:32 Reached Mt. Chengkeng, aka Mt. Sanjiao North Peak
12:02 Reached the wooden ladders
12:31 Found a great spot with wonderful views with my new friends for lunch. If I didn't miss Mt. Shuangfeng, it would be about time to reach it. It would also be about time to see the fork to Cihui Temple and Shuangfeng Road if I didn't miss it.
13:31 Resumed the hike
13:34 Reached Mt. Shuangfeng North Peak
14:14 Saw a stone with "0K 195M"
14:16 Reached Mt. Tunyuan
14:18 Passed Octagon Pavilion
14:27 Passed the fence with signs pointing to Hakka Yard 客家大院園區 and Tonglou Train Station
14:31 Exit the trail
Total time: 4 hours 43 minutes, including long breaks
